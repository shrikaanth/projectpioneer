Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our construction services in the GTA.

Do you serve the GTA?

Yes—Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Richmond Hill, Oakville, and nearby communities across the Greater Toronto Area.

Are your quotes detailed?

Yes—our quoting is built around clear scope and documented inclusions. You'll know exactly what's covered before work begins.

How do you handle changes?

Every change is documented with cost and schedule impact before approval. No surprises, no hidden costs.

Do you handle permits?

We coordinate the permit/inspection pathway as applicable to the scope. Code compliance is built into our planning process.

What's your service area in the GTA?

We serve Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Richmond Hill, Oakville, Burlington, Scarborough, North York, Etobicoke, and surrounding communities.

How long does a typical renovation take?

Timelines vary by scope. Basement renovations typically take 4-8 weeks, kitchen/bath renovations 2-6 weeks, and full home renovations 8-16 weeks. We provide milestone schedules during quoting.
